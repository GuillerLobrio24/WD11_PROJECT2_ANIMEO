Fairy Mountain

Synopsis

“Brahma”, a boy from the Eastern Kingdom, came to the Central Plains on a secret mission and lost some of his memory in a shipwreck. In order to repay the life-saving grace, Brahma agreed to the request of “Hongyin” Shitai, took Lu’er and Zhen to see the wonderful book, and went down the mountain to find the immortal island recorded in the book, cured Lu’er’s leg disease, and passed through Huancuiwan.

Synopsis
